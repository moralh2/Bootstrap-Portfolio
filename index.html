About Me

I moved to Austin about 4 years ago, looking for a change--more specifically, a career change. Within 3 months of moving here, I got a job as a QA in a big company. Working there, I learned a lot about general software testing, but more so, automated testing. This is what I went on to do at my second QA gig, this time at a smaller company. I've been at this job for about 2 years, and I was the only QA/QE up until some weeks ago.

Before moving to Austin, I lived in Baltimore for about 4 years. I initially went there to go to grad school, which I attended for about 2 years. While doing research, I realized that I enjoyed the coding aspect of what I was doing a lot more than the research itself. I eventually left graduate school, and started working part time jobs while applying to software-related ones. The process didn't pan out too well in Baltimore, so I moved here to try my luck again. And then I got my first real job in a software company.
